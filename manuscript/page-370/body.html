This constant selection of the best from the workers has impoverished those who are left, a sadly degraded remainder, for the great part, which, in the Ghetto, sinks to the deepest depths.  The wine of life has been drawn off to spill itself in blood and progeny over the rest of the earth.  Those that remain are the lees, and they are segregated and steeped in themselves.  They become indecent and bestial.  When they kill, they kill with their hands, and then stupidly surrender themselves to the executioners.  There is no splendid audacity about their transgressions.
