“But women,” I suggested, when he had finished proclaiming booze the sole end of existence.

“Wimmen!”  He thumped his pot upon the bar and orated eloquently.  “Wimmen is a thing my edication ’as learnt me t’ let alone.  It don’t pay, matey; it don’t pay.  Wot’s a man like me want o’ wimmen, eh? jest you tell me.
