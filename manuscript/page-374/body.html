But below this normal state of the average workman in town and country, there is found the great band of destitute outcasts—the camp followers of the army of industry—at least one-tenth the whole proletarian population, whose normal condition is one of sickening wretchedness.  If this is to be the permanent arrangement of modern society, civilization must be held to bring a curse on the great majority of mankind.
